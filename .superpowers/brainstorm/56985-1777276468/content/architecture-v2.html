Hard safety guarantees

Track A (intraday) is never modified. Track B requires all three LLM providers to be reachable before opening any new position — if Gemini, Groq, and OpenRouter all fail, an AIUnavailableError is raised and no trade fires. Existing open positions are not force-closed on AI failure — only new entries are blocked. The 40% capital ceiling for Track B is enforced at the allocator level, not inside individual signals.
