How should the AI reasoning layer work?

Deep analysis of news + congressional + social signals requires processing power. These are the free options.

A

Rule-Based Scoring FREE · FAST · RELIABLE

Weighted scoring formulas across all signals. Congressional buy on ticker X = +15 conviction. Negative GDELT tone on sector = −10. StockTwits bullish ratio >70% = +8. Scores combine into a per-ticker sentiment score that acts as the filter.

Example: NVDA score: +32 (congressional buy +15, GDELT AI regulation positive +12, StockTwits bullish +8) → ORB signal ALLOWED, confidence +10%

Pros

  • Zero API cost, zero latency
  • Fully auditable — see exactly why a score changed
  • Backtestable against historical signals

Cons

  • Can't understand nuance ("beat earnings but CFO resigned")
  • Requires manual weight tuning over time
B

Google Gemini Flash FREE · 1,500 REQ/DAY

Gemini 2.0 Flash free tier: 1,500 requests/day, 1M tokens/minute. Feed it a bundle of news + congressional + social context and get back a structured conviction score with full reasoning chain.

Example prompt: "Given these events about NVDA [congressional buy $250k, AI regulation news positive, StockTwits 74% bullish], rate market impact −10 to +10 with reasoning."

Pros

  • Understands nuance and complex relationships
  • Reasons: "Pelosi buys NVDA → AI committee chair → regulatory tailwind"
  • Free at trading volumes (dozens of analyses/day)

Cons

  • Adds 1–3s latency per analysis
  • Can hallucinate — needs output validation
  • Google dependency
C

Hybrid: Rules + Gemini Flash FREE · BEST OF BOTH

Rule-based scoring runs instantly for every signal. Gemini is called only when a high-conviction event is detected — large congressional trade, major political news, or sharp sentiment spike. Rules handle volume, LLM handles depth.

Logic: Rule score computed instantly → if score exceeds threshold → call Gemini for deep reasoning → merge both scores → final filter decision

Pros

  • Fast for routine signals, deep for high-impact events
  • Stays well within Gemini free tier (few LLM calls/day)
  • Auditable at both layers

Cons

  • More moving parts to build and test
  • Threshold tuning required to decide when to call LLM

My recommendation

C — Hybrid. Rules give instant auditable scoring for every event. Gemini Flash (free, 1,500 req/day) handles deep reasoning for high-conviction events like large congressional trades or major political news. Covers the full "deep reasoning" requirement at zero cost.
